Lending a Hand Abroad

Will Slotznick, a junior from West Chester, Pa., is studying
international relations in the College of Arts & Sciences with
minors in African studies and international development, and is
the founding director of Global Impact Collaborative (GIC). This
student-run initiative pairs undergraduates with international
nonprofits for pro bono research and consulting services around
issues of education, as well as monitoring and evaluation.
